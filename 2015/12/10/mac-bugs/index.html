Other questions

AppleID

AppleID and relevant informations should be kept in a safe place, in case that:

  1. You forget and lost these informations, then you cannot unlock your AppleID if it’s locked;
  2. Someone else get them, then he/she can use your card to busy apps at least;

Unfortunately, the first case happens to me recently. Then I cannot update my Iworks, even with my new created AppleID. I called Apple contact, he tell me to delete and reinstall Iworks.

Do not buy them directly in Appstore, Iworks should be free. Instead, the up-to-date service will give them to you freely, which needs your mac details.

If you buy them uncarefully as me, then you can go to Itunes -> view my account -> purchase history -> report a problem and select a reason and explain it.

Office

I must say it’s awful to use Office perfectly in MacOS, there is no clean and compatible solution. Afterward,

  1. Slides, Keynote
  2. Documents, MS Word
  3. Sheets, Google sheets online
  4. Only Read, Preview is enough?

I only use .doc/.docx or xls,xlsx when I apply for something, which is not too often. And usually, the format of xls,xlsx is too simple to worry about while the one of .doc/.docx is too hard to fit. I prefer to use Tex beamer for slides, but I want the amazing effect of Keynote one or two times.

So, we need to uninstall the softwares which are installed with the above ones together. To uninstall apps in MacOS, this post shows which directories we should have a look into. Microsoft has given the method to uninstall apps in Office already. OfficeThinner is a script to clean the same files in different Office apps, but you’d better use it carefully.
